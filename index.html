Why Seaward Labs

What we build

Seaward Labs is a small software company focused on practical tools for boaters. We build narrow products with a clear job to do, then refine them until they are useful in normal trip planning.

We use AI where it helps organize and summarize real information, not as decoration. The standard is simple: the software should make preparation clearer, calmer, and more efficient for people heading out on the water.
